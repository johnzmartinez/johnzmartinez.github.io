John Martinez – Bio

As a graduate student at University of California, Berkeley, I am currently teaching Math 10B with Prof. Stankova a graduate student instructor. I obtained a B.A. degree in Computer Science and a B.A. degree in Applied Math from the University of California, Berkeley in 2017. Afterward, I worked for eBay Inc. as a site reliability engineer/software engineer. Since 2016, I have been doing research in the visual computing lab on vision-correcting display under the supervision of Prof. Brian Barsky.
